The Opportunity

Alumni associations need a clearer picture of what is happening on the ground level. Groups want to engage more local alumni. Both lack the resources and digital toolset to be effective.

Problem

Consistency

"Which logo should I use on the website?" "Whats the hex code for Spartan Red? Wait, what is a hex code?" Groups are plagued by ugly unbranded websites that look like GeoCities circa 1998

Organization

"I add what to where?" "How do I track that?" "Who do I turn my receipt into?" "Did you get my email?" Some groups have to provide the same information to up to six different systems

Clarity

"Which group is performing the best?" "How many events happened last week and how well were they attended?" Only 45% of schools have strategic reporting tools in place. 1

Solution

A single platform that provides each level of an association the tools necessary to successfully serve their members.

Product

Symphony

Local groups create content

Then publicize their events with one click of a button

The alumni association sees everyone's activity
